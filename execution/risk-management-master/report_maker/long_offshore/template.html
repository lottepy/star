AQUMON Multi-factor Strategy Performance Report

Strategy Overview

AQUMON Multi-factor Strategy is a combination of fundamental analysis and state-of-the-art AI techniques. By diving into company characteristics, potential stocks can be selected out of more than three thousand stocks in China A-share market. Company characteristics are quantified as a series of factors which describe company styles in different dimensions. These factors could be value factor, growth factor, momentum factor, volatility factor and so on.

Key Facts

Asset Class: Equity

Base Currency: CNY

Benchmark: CSI300 Index, CSI500 Index
