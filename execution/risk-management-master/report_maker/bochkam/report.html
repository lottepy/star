Investment Philosophy

AQUMON-BOCHKAM Global Asset Allocation strategy is derived from AQUMON SmartGlobal® Framework. AQUMON SmartGlobal® Framework selects high-quality underlying assets and allocate precisely to achieve high diversification and low downside risk. Dynamic risk analysis and portfolio optimization mechanics are in place to ensure the stability and quality of returns. Besides, in order to enhance the performance, this stragegy also applies several machine learning techniques to capture the mean reversion features in equity markets.

About Magnum Research

Incubated by the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology in 2016, Magnum Research secured its A Round funding led by Alibaba Entrepreneurs Fund in Jun 2017, which was later (Sep 2018) followed by funding from Bohai XingWang, L.P., managed by BOC International.
